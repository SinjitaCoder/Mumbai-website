Mumbai- The City of Dreams

About

Mumbai (formerly called Bombay) is a densely populated city on India’s west coast. A financial center, it's India's largest city. On the Mumbai Harbour waterfront stands the iconic Gateway of India stone arch, built by the British Raj in 1924. Offshore, nearby Elephanta Island holds ancient cave temples dedicated to the Hindu god Shiva. The city's also famous as the heart of the Bollywood film industry. City of Dreams, Maximum City, Economic Capital of India; there are several tags attached when it comes to definition of Mumbai city in the state of Maharashtra. ... the city which never sleeps… Mumbai never disappoints and so does its' streets bustling with activity and the city sells dreams to everyone
